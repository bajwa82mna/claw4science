Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Claw4Science (claw4science.campusinchina.com) is a static website with no user accounts, no login, and no user data collection. This page describes our minimal data practices.

Data We Collect

This website does not collect personal data. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts that collect user-identifiable information. Our hosting infrastructure (GitHub Pages) may collect standard server log data (IP addresses, request times, user agents) as part of normal web server operation. This is not within our control.

Third-Party Links

This website links to third-party services including GitHub, arXiv, and individual project websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. When you click external links, you are subject to the privacy policies of those sites.

Changes

We may update this policy. Any changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date at the top of this page.
